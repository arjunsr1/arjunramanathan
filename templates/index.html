Who am I?

I’m Arjun Ramanathan, and I am currently a software Engineer at Cisco Systems. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, and I’ve been surrounded by technology my entire life. I was drawn to computer science and software engineering mainly by the exciting feeling of being able to come up with unique solutions to common problems, thus giving me nearly full autonomy in how I approach a problem. After my first intro computer science course during undergrad, I’ve never looked back. My interest within the realm of software engineering is primarily on developing or working on services and platforms that people can use on a day to day basis.
