Who am I?

I’m Arjun Ramanathan, and I am currently a Software Engineer at Cisco Systems. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, and I’ve been surrounded by technology my entire life. I was drawn to computer science and software engineering mainly by the exciting feeling of being able to come up with unique solutions to common problems. After my first introductory Computer Science course during my time in undergrad, I’ve never looked back. My interest within the realm of Software Engineering is primarily centered on developing and working on services and platforms that people can use on a day to day basis. Keep scrolling to learn a bit more about me!





Education

I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2020 with a B.S. in Computer Science. It was at Michigan that I got my first exposure to the field of Computer Science, and it quickly became my major of choice. Some relevant coursework I completed is listed here: Operating Systems, Databases, Web Systems, Intro to Artificial Intelligence, Computer Security.





Technology Interests
